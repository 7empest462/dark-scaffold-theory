## Step-by-Step arXiv Submission

### Step 1: Create arXiv Account (if needed)

1. Go to **https://arxiv.org/user/register**
2. Fill in your details:
   - Name: Rob Simens
   - Email: rob@simens.io
   - Affiliation: Independent Researcher
3. Verify email and complete registration

### Step 2: Start New Submission

1. Log in at **https://arxiv.org/user/login**
2. Click **"Start New Submission"** (top right)
3. Choose submission type: **"New submission"**

### Step 3: Select Category

**Primary category:** `astro-ph.CO` (Cosmology and Nongalactic Astrophysics)

**Secondary categories (optional):**

- `gr-qc` (General Relativity and Quantum Cosmology)
- `hep-th` (High Energy Physics - Theory)

### Step 4: Upload Files

1. Click **"Upload files"**
2. Choose **"tar.gz archive"** upload method
3. Upload: `arxiv_submission.tar.gz`
4. arXiv will automatically compile your LaTeX and show a preview

### Step 5: Preview and Verify

1. Check the PDF preview
2. Verify all figures appear correctly
3. Verify equations render properly
4. Check for any LaTeX errors in the log

### Step 6: Enter Metadata

**Title:**

```
The Pre-Existing Dark Scaffold: A Unified Framework for Dark Matter, Dark Energy, and Emergent Gravity
```

**Authors:**

```
Rob Simens
```

**Abstract:** (Copy from paper.tex, lines 21-23)

**Comments:**

```
5 pages, 8 figures. Submitted to Physical Review D
```

**Report number:** (Leave blank for independent work)

### Step 7: Submit

1. Review all information
2. Agree to arXiv policies
3. Click **"Submit"**

### Step 8: Wait for Processing

- arXiv reviews new submissions daily
- First submissions take ~1-2 business days for moderation
- You'll receive email confirmation when posted

## After arXiv Posting

### Get Your arXiv ID

Your paper will be assigned an ID like: `arXiv:2602.XXXXX`

Update `paper.tex` line 11 with this ID for journal submission.

### Submit to Journal (Optional)

**Physical Review D:**

1. Go to https://journals.aps.org/prd/authors
2. Create account
3. Submit via their online system
4. Reference your arXiv ID

**JCAP (faster review):**

1. Go to https://jcap.sissa.it/
2. Submit via ScholarOne
3. Include arXiv link

## Important Notes

- **Do NOT** upload `paper.pdf` — arXiv compiles from `.tex`
- **DO** include all figures in the `figures/` folder
- arXiv is free and open-access
- Journal submission is separate and usually has fees ($1000-2000 for PRD)
